A Place Within You

Rising from the scenic foothills of Southwest Reno and lit by the moonlight of the expansive western sky, an exclusive residential enclave captures the mystique of its name: Marama.

A lyrical word in the Maori language, Marama is layered with the meaning of moon, wisdom, and the light of the world. It describes soaring elevations, stunning views, and wide-open nature.

Marama, perched at the shoulder of the Sierra Nevadas and surrounded by sculptured boulders, waving grasses, and desert blooms, offers a limited collection of custom estate lots where homes aren’t merely built on the land—they are stitched into the landscape.
